Events and updates

Jupiter Ascending

06-03-2015

Join us when we screen Jupiter Ascending. A sci-fi movie with plenty of action and adventure. See Channing Tantum and Mila Kunis fight off aliens together!

Chappie

13-03-2015

You may know them as the duo Die Antwoord but this year they are on the big screen. Together with Dev Patel and Hugh Jackman, they reboot a robot and give it a conscious. A warming action movie worth seeing!

Birdman

20-03-2015

Washed-up Riggan who used to play an iconic superhero fights to become something else than Birdman. Trying to establish himself as a true artist with a new Broadway show he meets a lot of struggle. If you need a good laugh you should come and see it at ITU.Film!

John Wick

27-03-2015

An ex-hitman wants to track down the gangsters that took everything from him. Keanu Reeves is a bad-ass and nothing will stop him...

Big Hero 6

03-04-2015

Robotics nerd Hiro Hamada discovers an inflatable health care robot Baymax created in the past by his brother, Tedashi. After a terrible life-changing accident, Hiro and Baymax team up with four other nerds and save their hometown San Fransokyo from an evil super villain trying to take over with Hiro's invention.

Interstellar

10-04-2015

Earth's future has been riddled by disasters, famines, and droughts. There is only one way to ensure mankind's survival: Interstellar travel. A newly discovered wormhole in the far reaches of our solar system allows a team of astronauts to go where no man has gone before, a planet that may have the right environment to sustain human life.
