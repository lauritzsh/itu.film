About ITU.Film

ITU.Film is ITU’s student driven cinema. We screen moving pictures whether it be documentaries, movies, series or films. For the past several years screenings have taken place in cozy cafe Analog.

As orginazation, ITU.film is small and build up of movie loving students.

If you are interested in film and free popcorn, like us on Facebook so you won't miss an event.

See you at the next screening!
